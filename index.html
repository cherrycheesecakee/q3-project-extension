Falling Behind

"I wrote “Falling Behind” when I had just moved out to LA and I felt like all the people around me were falling in love and I couldn’t help but feel like I was falling behind. With bossa nova inspirations, harmony stacks and modern production, I hope that “Falling Behind” is a hopeless romantic’s summer anthem." -Laufey

Valentine

"“Valentine” has this funny, off, almost-drunk pacing to it. The last line: “I blinked and suddenly I found a Valentine”, is a resolution. It’s holding for a time, and then it resolves in the end. That resolution feels very satisfying, it feels like something finishing or something making sense." -Laufey
